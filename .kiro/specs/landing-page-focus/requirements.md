# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Refactor the landing page to focus on Smart POS as the primary conversion goal. De-emphasize non-POS services and rewrite benefits using Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS) copywriting framework.

## Glossary

- **Smart POS**: The flagship AI-powered point of sale system
- **PAS Framework**: Problem-Agitate-Solve copywriting technique that presents pain points before solutions
- **Services Section**: The section displaying business offerings (currently 3 equal cards)
- **Benefits Section**: The "What This Means for Your Business" section

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a landing page visitor, I want to see Smart POS prominently featured, so that I understand it's the main product offering.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor views the Services section THEN the Smart POS card SHALL be visually larger and more prominent than other service mentions
2. WHEN non-POS services are displayed THEN the Services section SHALL show them in a condensed, de-emphasized format below the main POS card
3. WHEN a visitor looks for other services THEN the Services section SHALL provide a simple link or mention directing to contact

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a landing page visitor, I want benefits presented with relatable problems first, so that I emotionally connect with the solutions offered.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor reads a benefit THEN the Benefits section SHALL present a problem statement first using conversational language
2. WHEN presenting the solution THEN the Benefits section SHALL follow the problem with a clear resolution tied to Smart POS
3. WHEN displaying benefit content THEN the Benefits section SHALL maintain the 4-card grid layout with updated PAS copy
